Storage and performance are things you don’t have to worry about with the Samsung J6 Plus. Featuring a powerful Snapdragon processor with 4GB of RAM, 64GB of ROM which is expandable up to 512 GB, this smartphone lets you multitask and store as much content as you want. The Samsung J6 Plus is also available in a range of cool colors.
